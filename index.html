I am a Research Scientist at Shanghai AI Laboratory (shlab) working with Prof. Dahua Lin. I hold a Ph.D. degree in Computer Science from Hong Kong University of Science and Technology under the supervision of Prof. Huamin Qu.

My research interest lies in the broad field of Responsible AI, an interdisciplinary topic, which combines machine learning, human-computer interaction, computational social science and data visualization. Currently, I am particularly interested in enhancing the interpretability, safety, robustness, fairness and privacy of large language models.
